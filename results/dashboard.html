Why Not Accuracy?

The dataset has severe class imbalance:
91.9% repaid  |  8.1% didn't repay

A model that predicts everyone repays gets 91.9% accuracy but is completely useless — it never catches a risky client.

AUC-ROC measures whether the model correctly ranks risky clients above safe ones, regardless of threshold. A random model = 0.50. Our model = 0.79.
